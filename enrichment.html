Lam Learning Hub Enrichment Courses

At Lam Learning Hub, we believe that although grades can get a child far, it is interest and passion that sustains learning through life. When children find a subject interesting, they see studying and homework as a challenge to learn and know more, instead of a mundane chore. We are here, to unleash that interest.

We offer two kinds of Enrichment workshops,

Get your A's!
These workshops reinforce your child's knowledge and understanding of school curriculum through our creative non-textbook methods.
Learning through exploring!
These workshops aim to make learning fun by taking learning outside of the school syllabus and exposing your child to interesting applications of the subjects they learn in school.

English Fun Series - Literacy and Art Fun

Objective
By drawing inspiration from Art to learn English, we make learning the English language interesting.
Outline
We teach children to draw a picture to create a story. They will get to learn the words and phrases associated with the picture. Finally, children will be guided to write sentences comprising these new words.
Age Group
7 to 10
Date
June 2012

English Fun Series - Vocabulary Fun

Objective
Building strong vocabulary through stories and games.
Outline
The lack of vocabulary hinders children's ability to write good compositions. Through story telling and games, we teach antonyms, synonyms and related adjectives and adverbs to help build a stronger command of vocabulary.
Age Group
7 to 10
Date
June 2012

Maths Fun Series - Measurement Fun
