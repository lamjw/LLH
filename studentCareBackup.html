Little Professors Student Care Services

At Little Professors Hougang Student Care, we believe in preparing your child for life. We want to develop in your child the socio-emotional, intellectual and creative thinking skills needed to do well when they grow up. We want your children to be pioneers, thinkers and humanitarians.

We develop these skills by exposing your child to ideas, developing your child's character, and bringing out the best in your child academically, through a safe and supportive environment.

The Learning Experience

Every child in Little Professors Hougang will get a learning experience in line with our philosophy of developing ideas, character and academics through a supportive environment.

Ideas

Library :: We have an up-to-date range of mind stimulating books like National Geographic Kids®, Reader's Digest®; and Discovery BOX®.

Educational Games :: We stock the best-selling miniLUK Brain Challenger® series, a game that trains math through counting money, and many more. Your child can even construct his or her own vocabulary games here!

Holiday Programmes :: Everything from poetry to chinese chess to sudoku to fun science experiments! Did we mention fun and informative excursions?

Character

Well-behaved Students :: Our students are polite and well-behaved. We want our students to be themselves and speak freely, but we have a zero-tolerance stand against offensive language.

Behaviour Incentive System :: Your child will be awarded with points for good behaviour and performance. Points are deducted for inappropriate behaviour. These points can then be exchanged for prizes.

Civics and Moral Education :: We hold CME lessons for your child to instill sound ethics, and promote a positive attitude. These CME lessons are conducted interactively (eg. through role-play) to engage the children.

Academics

Qualified Teachers :: Our student care teachers are very well-qualified. Several are university graduates. All of our student care teachers hold at least a polytechnic diploma or GCE 'A' Level Certificate.

In-house Tuition :: Your child will be grouped with other children of the same level and given tuition after completing his or her homework. The quality of in-house tuition is assured by the quality of our teachers.

Results :: Our model has improved the school results of almost all our students. We don't expect you to believe us by giving you only a few of such examples. Visit us for more details!

Environment

Clean Environment :: We maintain a clean and dry environment. We do not use carpets as they absorb moisture on rainy days, causing a stench.

We provide Before School Care for students attending school in the afternoon and After School Care for those attending school in the morning.

Before School Care

Time Activity Description
0700-0900 Arrival / Breakfast / Reading Children will have their breakfast upon arrival.
0900-1130 Homework / In-house Tuition Children will complete their homework first, after which in-house tuition and additional assessment will be given.
1130-1200 Lunch Time to rest and eat!
After 1200 Depart for school Time to go to school!

After School Care

Time Activity Description
1315-1415 Arrival / Lunch Children will have their breakfast upon arrival.
1415-1600 Homework / Rest & Relax Children will complete their homework first, and get to rest after they are done.
1600-1630 Tea Break Time to rest and eat!
1630-1800 In-house Tuition Children are grouped into respective levels and given in-house tuition and additional assessments.
1800-1830 Educational Play / Television Board Games, Free Play, Reading
After 1830 Depart for Home Home Sweet Home
