Welcome to Little Professors Hougang | Global Art Hougang

We are a small enterprise in primary school services in Hougang. We provide Student Care, Tuition, Enrichment and Art & Craft for primary school children.

Despite our short heritage originating in 2008, we currently have over a hundred students. We have seen many of our students develop into morally upright individuals with a strong character and outspoken personality. We have seen them develop into independent thinkers and team players.

Our model for success? We nurture their character. We encourage them to generate ideas. We provide the environment for them to do well at school, and they do.
