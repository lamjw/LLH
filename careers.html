Careers

Do you

  • believe in your ability to help your students do better academically?
  • believe in building the character of children from young?
  • feel very satisfied when your students proudly show you their achievements?
  • have a passion in teaching?
  • actively find ways to improve your teaching?

If you do, we invite you to join us! We have the following job openings:

Part Time (Morning) Student Care Teacher cum Admin

As a forward looking student care cum tuition centre, we are in a unique position to combine character and academic development under one roof. We have strong track records from our students.

Besides, we offer a wide variety of enrichment activities and programs during school holidays to ensure learning is fun. As part of the teaching team, you have ample opportunities to carry out your dreamed programmes.

University undergraduates, 'A' level and diploma holders are welcome to apply for the part-time positions. The hands-on experience you gain will be invaluable in helping you decide if you should pursue full time teaching as a career. Stay-home mums are welcomed to apply for the part-time position too. You will certainly pick up lots of useful tips in educating your own children.

Responsibilities
  • Commitment to teaching and character development of children
  • Plan and prepare lessons as per curriculum
  • Supervise and coach students academically
  • Classroom management
  • Centre Administration
  • Assist centre manager in preparation of activities
    • Requirements
  • Willing to learn, take initiative and must have a strong sense of responsibility
  • Good command of English
  • Able to teach primary school subjects
  • Minimally Diploma or "A" Level holders
  • Experience is an advantage, but training will be provided
  • Must be able to work the following shift: 7.30am - 1pm
