Little Professors Hougang Tuition Services

Little Professors Hougang offers tuition for primary school students. We believe that all students should be given an opportunity to think and discover for themselves.

Our teachers strive to inspire and motivate students to form their own understanding of the topic or subject. By doing so, our students are trained to reason and create independently the concept they learn.

With a team of passionate teachers trained in our pedagogy, coupled with a small class size, we are confident in sharpening your child's mind.

The Learning Experience

Every tuition student in Little Professors Hougang will get a learning experience derived upon good pedagogy and delivery.

Pedagogy

Problem Solving
Skills

We train our students to solve problems. Primary school syllabus is often taught in schools by topic. However, one reason why students do poorly is that they do not realize several problem solving skills are reused across many topics. We emphasize this connection to our students.

Ask & Learn

We believe in learning by asking. This is the only way a student can go through the thought process him/herself. If a student does not ask questions, we inspire him/her by asking questions instead. We want our students to discover concepts themselves.

Expression

Written communication is an essential skill in school and in life. We teach our students how to answer questions, because we believe they should not be penalized for concepts they understand.

Delivery

Passionate Teachers

We only select passionate teachers. These are teachers that have participated in activities outside their course of study that indicate an interest in teaching or the subject they teach.

Attention

Our classes are small, with an average size of four students per class. This allows our teachers to pay more attention to your child.

Trained Teachers

We train our teachers in our pedagogy, classroom management, and primary school syllabus. Although not every of our teachers worked with MOE, we believe that our training programme produces competent teachers that are adept at teaching small classes.
